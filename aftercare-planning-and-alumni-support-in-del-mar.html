Frequently Asked Questions About Aftercare Planning and Alumni Support

What does the aftercare planning process at Villa Kali Ma Outpatient Treatment for Women in Del Mar involve?
Our aftercare planning process begins as you near the end of your outpatient treatment. This can include finding local support groups around Del Mar, connecting with therapists or psychiatrists, and setting up relapse prevention strategies. We also help coordinate family support and recommend holistic options, such as yoga or mindfulness classes available in the area.
How much does alumni support cost, and is there a membership fee?
Alumni support at Villa Kali Ma is completely complimentary for all graduates of our outpatient programs. There is no membership fee. This includes access to monthly alumni meetings, social events in Del Mar, and ongoing check-ins from our team. Is Villa Kali Ma licensed to provide outpatient treatment and aftercare in California?
Yes, Villa Kali Ma Outpatient Treatment for Women is fully licensed and certified by the California Department of Health Care Services (DHCS) to provide outpatient substance use and mental health treatment. We encourage regular participation in alumni activities and check-ins, especially during your first year post-treatment, but you can access our support network and resources whenever you need them.
Can you guarantee that I won’t relapse if I participate in your aftercare and alumni support programs?
